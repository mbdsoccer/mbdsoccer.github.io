Know your Waffle!

Here we can see an image of a piquant waffle. As all of us surely know waffles can even turn the most cantankerous people into saints. This great food item, that fits into any meal of the day and is made from quaility ingreditents like eggs, milk, butter, and vanilla. Here we will be reviewing different tpyes and quailities of waffles, for your ultimate waffle expirience.

The Blueberry Waffle

Here we can see one of the most well known waffles, and just so happens to be my favorite, the coveted blueberry waffle. It is disctincted by either blueberrys baked in, blueberrys on top, or by blueberry syrup. It is commonly known to appear at breakfeast time or at waffle house.

The Strawberry Waffle

Even though the strawberry could be baked into the waffle with flavoring, strawberrys are a great waffle topping that go with most other waffles. They can be used to make your waffle more appealing to the eye or if your just in the mood to feel like royalty.

The Chocolate Chip Waffle

Like other waffles we have gone over, the chocolate can either be bake in or sprinkled in on top. If you dont have a plain waffle it will most likely be chocolate chip. The chips add a richness to the waffle that you cant get from adding fruit. And the taste will change completely depending on what type of chocolate you use.

The OG Waffle

This is the very first original waffle. Its is like your canvas waiting for it to be turned into a perfect masterpiece. This waffle is not limited to its base, it will be cultivated in many ways by many people until it ascends to a higher state of perfection.
